How to get to Gili Island?

From Lombok Airport
We recommend access from Lombok Airport for those of you who want to vacation in Lombok and also visit Gili Island. You can depart from Lombok Airport via the Bypass route then head to Mataram City and arrive in Senggigi then from Senggigi you will head to Bangsal Harbor or Teluk Nare. At this port you will board a boat to cross to Gili Island in about 20 minutes. If you don't want the hassle, you can order a Lombok Tour Package which includes a tour to Gili Island at Lombok Local Area Travel.
See flight ticket to Lombok


From Bali Island
When you have finished enjoying your holiday in Bali, you can occasionally try a tour to Gili Island departing from the island of Bali. You can go to Padang Bay Harbor then take a fast boat that goes to Gili at a cost of around 350,000/one way/person and the time taken by sea to arrive is around 2 hours.

From Lembar Harbor, Lombok
For those of you backpackers who usually come from Lembar Harbor, you can take public transportation which routes from Lembar Harbor to Senggigi then from Senggigi you can take the Sutle Bus which goes to Bangsal Port or Teluk Nare and take a boat to Gili island.

This is the route from Lombok International Airport to Bangsal Port. You can drag the marker to see route from another places.

About Gili

The Gili Islands (Indonesian: Tiga Gili [Three Gilis], Kepulauan Gili [Gili Islands]) are an archipelago of three small islands or Gili island triplets — Gili Trawangan, Gili Meno and Gili Air — just off the northwest coast of Lombok, Indonesia. The local name of these three islands is Gili Indah which means "small beautiful islands".
